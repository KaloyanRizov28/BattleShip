Welcome to Battleship

Prepare for an epic naval battle! Are you ready to sink your opponent's fleet?

How to Play:

  1. Place your ships by dragging and dropping.
  2. Your fleet is stationed on the left grid with your ships in position
  3. Take aim at the enemy grid (right side) by clicking to fire
  4. Wait for the enemy's counter-attack after your turn
  5. First to sink all enemy ships wins the battle

Battle Legend:

Your Ships - Fleet Position
Miss - Shot Hit Water
Hit - Direct Strike!

Combat Tips:

  • When you score a hit, target adjacent squares to find the rest of the ship
  • The enemy will focus their fire around successful hits
  • Already targeted locations cannot be fired upon again
  • The grid is locked during enemy turns - stay alert!
